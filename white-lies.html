Graphic Designer

WHITE LIES ENTERTAINMENT

White Lies Entertainment is an event planning and dj hiring business. For their upcoming event, Winter Bassdown they hired me to design and create a poster and banner to advertise the event.

The Goal

White Lies Entertainment wanted me to create a poster and a banner to advertise their upcoming event, Winter Bassdown. They gave me mostly creative freedom on the design, however it did have to follow their layout. I had a time frame of roughly two weeks.

Sketches

I started by researching other event posters, mostly for DNB and rock gigs. I also researched blue colour schemes and how other posters used a blue theme in their design. After researching, I sketched several ideas I had for the design.

Colour Scheme

I chose a blue theme because it conveyed the winter theme of the event. By using a highlighter blue against a dark blue I was able to create more contrast and depth with similar colours. The dark blue creates a night feeling, and the highlighter blue looks like neon lights which both reflected the event.

Title Design

For the title, I was inspired by slime text because it is very reminiscent of dark, and grungy design. To stay with the winter theme, I created a frozen text inspired by the dripping slime design.

Font Design

I used Frankling Gothic Medium for the font.

For the header text I used a white fill and added the same text over top with no fill and a blue stroke to create the 3D effect that matched the title text.

For the sub text, I used a white a blue gradient so that the act names stood out, and for the important information I used plain white text so that it was easy to read.

Final Design
