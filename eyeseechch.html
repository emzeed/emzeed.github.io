EYE SEE CHCH

Role

UX & UI Designer

Web Developer

Link

Click Me

EYE SEE CHCH is a photosharing app that allows users to upload and interact with posts focused on Christchurch.

It was created by myself and two other students for an assignment while studying at Yoobee School of Design.

The Goal

We wanted the app to have an unconventional and streetstyle-like design while still using basic UI principles to create an easy to navigate and use interface.

Concept Design

The theme of our app consisted of looking at pictures of Christchurch. From this, we chose a red colour scheme to represent Christchurch, and the symbol of an eye to representing “looking” at Christchurch.

All of the illustrations and assets were designed and created by the team.
