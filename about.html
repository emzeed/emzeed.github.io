My name's Emma Dodd. I'm a web developer and designer living in wonderful New Zealand.

Since I was a teenager I have been freelancing websites and design work.

In 2020, I studied a Diploma of Web & UX Design after some years of self teaching.

I love wacky, wonderful design that doesn’t follow the rules, without sacrificing the principles of design.

In my spare time you’ll find me painting, doodling, or looking for a bearded lizard to adopt.
