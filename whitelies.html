WHITE LIES ENTERTAINMENT

Role

Graphic Designer

White Lies Entertainment is an event management businesses. For their upcoming , Winer Bassdown they needed a poster and banner.

The Goal

The goal was to create a design in a poster form and banner form. The only design request was that it followed the layout of their previous designs.

Concept Design

To follow the winter night theme of the event, I used a blue colour palette. The title text was inspired by slime text which I found in my research. I reimagined this as frozen text to create the final design.
